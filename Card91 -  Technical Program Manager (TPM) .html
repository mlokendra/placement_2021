Company : Card91
Company Name : Card91
Nature Of Business : Tech Infra - Card Issuance Platform - and B2B Payments
Designation : Technical Program Manager (TPM)
Tentative Job Location : Bangalore
Description :

Company Profile

Card91 is simplifying business payments by providing a plug and play issuance infrastructure to businesses for domestic and cross-border payments. Our full stack platform enables businesses to gain control and visibility into their payment flows, controlling the entire payment lifecycle from onboarding, to issuance, to transaction and data reconciliation. The platform aims to provide holistic solutions around payment issuance and distribution management. Our deep focus in payments will help us in bringing new issuance formats as well as technologies to businesses.

The company has been founded by seasoned technology entrepreneurs who have founded successful companies like Myntra, Mastiff Tech earlier. Headquartered in Bangalore, with presence in Mumbai and NCR, Card91 aims to disrupt the payment infrastructure space in the issuer-processor segment.

Position: Technical Program Manager (TPM)

As soon as a business client sales cycle is complete the work of Technical Program Manager starts. A TPM is a single point of contact and is expected to take the client from “after sales'' to “Live deployment”. TPM’s are supposed to handle at least a few single digit clients at one time.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Card91’s SPOC for the client’s business, technical team
  • Coordination between client’s business, technical and Card91’s business, technical & design team
  • Provide detailed API explanation and Sandbox / Live business portal’s demonstrations to the client’s teams.
  • Daily coordination with multiple teams on the client’s and Card91’s end to facilitate seamless integration and testing
  • Supervise the testing on different platforms, QA, UAT and sign off the client’s api’s integration

Experience and Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering a must. MBA students with relevant tech background are also eligible.
  • 1-2 years of experience in Tech System / API integration & implementation
  • Ability to work and effectively communicate with other teams (external or internal) on online or offline mode
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality while handling sensitive information
  • Financial services experience is a plus


What’s on offer

  • Vibrant, fun and rewarding culture that nurtures and promotes excellence
  • Opportunities to learn and interact with payment industry leaders
  • Competitive remuneration, group health insurance & PF
  • Other office perks of being part of an early-stage Start-up
  • Offsite travel (in India to start with) to the corporate office of a business client.
  • Location: HSR Layout, Bangalore
