Company : Agri10x
Company Name : Agri10x
Nature Of Business : Agteh
Designation : Data Scientist
Tentative Job Location : Pune
Description :

Position Summary

The ideal candidate is a team player who will be responsible for working with company data in various business areas. Specific responsibilities include reporting metrics, analysing methodologies, suggesting operation improvements, and building proposal evaluations in a cross-functional environment.

Do you love sifting through large amounts of data? Are you enthusiastic in developing algorithms that can handle terabytes of data? If so, Agri10x is looking for innovative thinkers and problem solvers to help our customers create consumer value at every touchpoint by building and supporting cutting-edge marketing data science solutions.

We are growing fast and need brilliant Data Scientists like you to fuel our continuing innovation and help us scale. Along the way, you will find exceptional growth opportunities limited only by your hunger for learning and ability to apply best practice methodology in our start-up-like environment.

What You Will Be Doing

As a Data Scientist, you will be tasked with solving data problems end to end. This Means:

  • Track and report data
  • Build cross-functional partnerships, internally and externally
  • MIS & Reports
  • Research on competitive market knowledge
  • Reports in excel sheet on day-to-day basis and reporting to Management.
  • Identifying, developing and executing data analysis to help marketing, product and category functions create more business impact.
  • Presenting written recommendations and insights to key stakeholders.
  • Shaping and informing key metrics and indicators by which leadership will assess its performance.
  • Learn and understand a broad range of Amazon’s data resources and know how, when and which data sources to use.
  • Deep dive into massive data sets to answer key business questions using MS Excel, SQL and other data manipulation languages. Basic Qualifications
  • Ability to work effectively & independently in a fast-paced environment with tight deadlines.
  • Ability to engage with cross-functional teams for implementation of project and program requirements.
  • To be able to provide view & support clients as required in areas like Productivity, Cost Reduction and Data driven analytics etc.
  • Experience in creating Analytical dashboards and data visualization using Tableau.
  • Experience in report writing, writing and running database queries and data analysis.
  • Managing data loads, security, and integrity of a data mart
  • Analyse and identify red flags in the overall financial and operational performance, and propose ways to bridge them (Learning, Process & other Initiatives).
  • Bring closure with accurate reconciliation between Management MIS, Org P&L and Operational Dashboards while working closely with the financial operations team.
  • Lead/ participate and actively drive new initiatives and pilot standardization, automation projects with the relevant Product/ Tech teams for process improvement and execution efficiency

Functional Competencies

  • Problem solving: For the position to be successful, the Candidate must be capable of solving for an unclear business challenge with various data streams affecting decision-making.
  • Data driven/ analytical: The employee must be comfortable processing and analysing large amounts of data from different sources in order to fulfil corporate objectives.
  • Collaborative skills: To be successful, this position demands a lot of cross-functional collaboration and interaction with top executives. A high-growth, youthful, and agile company environment requires the ability to handle these interpersonal dynamics with ease.
  • Results orientation: Business and efficacy expects this job to deliver on daily figures, short-term targets, and long-term milestones. To be successful, you will need to reach these goals.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree/ B. Stat/ M. Stat  or equivalent experience
  • The ability to articulate and translate business questions and use statistical techniques to resolve data analysis problems
  • Knowledge of statistical data analysis such as linear models, multivariate analysis, stochastic models and sampling methods
  • Experience with machine learning on large datasets and in deploying machine learning models into a production environment
  • Excellent Excel and SQL knowledge and, ability to navigate large volumes of data.
  • Statistical software programming and models (ie. SAS, SQL, HIVE, Python or equivalent) skills required
  • Advanced Excel (including VBA, Macros, PowerPivot, formula and modelling),SharePoint and PowerPoint skills require
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
