About the Company

Decision Point is a fast-growing Consumer Products Analytics & Consulting Company, with business partners featuring in global fortune 500 list. We specialize in applying math to solve complex business problems in CPG space.

DP assures you to provide a 360degree learning platform which will be steepest in the industry, you will develop in-depth know-how of CPG/FMCG business, learn the application of analytics and big data technologies and traits of management consulting.

Decision Point offers an entrepreneurial environment and exposure to advanced analytics in the domain of sales and marketing. The team at Decision Point is very young, energetic and highly skilled. Our team consists of talented business consultants, data scientists and engineers who are passionate about implementing analytics in real world business problems

A career at Decision Point is rich in experience and offers opportunities to build lasting relationships.

Roles and Responsibilities

The successful candidate will be part of Analytics Consulting team. Business analyst plays a critical role in team. BA is responsible for conducting analyses, developing mathematical models and building knowledge on CPG industry. The incumbent for the position is expected to deliver but not limited to on following responsibilities

  • Manage large datasets using most recent technologies
  • Continuously improve processes with focus on automation and partner with different teams to develop system capability
  • Partner with project leads to understand business briefs and execute analysis
  • Develop and enhance statistical model with best in class modelling techniques
  • Evaluate new data sources, conduct research to assist in solving business problems
  • Communicate with clients to take briefs or present insights (need driven)
  • Deliver on informative and well-organized deliverables
  • Proactively seek opportunities to help team members by sharing knowledge and expanding skills

An ideal candidate will be/have:

  • Experience of working on Big data platforms - Big query, MS Azure
  • Have good grip over excel
  • Has firsthand working knowledge of programming languages R, Python
