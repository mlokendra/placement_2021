About (ICG Analytics Center)

ICG Analytics Group (ICG-A) was created in late 2008 to provide senior management a client
level view across ICG businesses and to better identify potential client level opportunities. ICG-
A provides with consistent client metrics, segmentation and insights across ICG businesses and
regions. ICG-A is responsible for implementation of cross-divisional client metrics including
revenue, profitability and market share, as well as client segmentation based on opportunity,
client feedback and client content based on event probabilities. The team has developed an
analytical platform to aggregate multiple internal and external data sources and integrate the
data by client on multiple dimensions including equity and debt performance, fundamentals,
institutional ownership and analyst estimates. It also segments and profiles clients in relation to
their respective peer groups based on industry, style, size and region.


Role Outline/Job Summary
This position, which is part of ICG – Analytics statistical team, is responsible for many ICG level
analytical projects across business lines.
The position will be responsible to support / define many senior management strategic initiatives
and back it by data.
In – order to achieve above, specific accountability will include, but not limited to
− Work with large and complex data sets (both internal and external data) to conduct analyses
− Develop market/product/client insights to help senior management make business decisions
− Design and create performance metrics by business, region, industry, and product for
periodic business reviews
− Identify and compile data sets using a variety of tools (e.g. SQL, Python) to help predict,
improve, and measure the success of key business to business outcomes
− Articulate analytical findings in clear and concise deliverables, including presentations,
discussions, and visualizations
− Responsible for documenting data requirements, data collection / processing / cleaning, and
exploratory data analysis; which may include utilizing statistical models / algorithms and data
visualization techniques
