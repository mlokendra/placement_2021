About QA Model Monitoring
Model Monitoring acts as one of the primary ways to mitigate Model Risk by highlighting areas of sub-optimal
performance and by proposing effective remediation plan for models in use by business. Model Risk is the potential
for adverse consequences (e.g. financial loss, reputational impact, regulatory censure etc.) from decisions based on
incorrect or misused model outputs and reports. This can arise from fundamental model weaknesses leading to
inaccurate outputs, errors in implementation, or incorrect/inappropriate use. Monitoring team periodically evaluate
model performance. Monitoring findings help business to decide upon the further uses of the model in order to
Continue, Adjust and then Continue or discontinue and Rebuild the Model.
