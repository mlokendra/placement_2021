Job description --> Engineer – Research and Technology

Team: Airframe – Research and Technology

The R&T team is a part of the ‘stress and design’ group under Airframe department and plays a key role in demonstrating emerging technology readiness levels and developing innovative solutions for design and development of current and future aircraft programmes.

Job scope (core activities):

  • Evaluate emerging technologies and demonstrate their readiness level for aircraft applications
  • Propose new architectures, concepts and explore disruptive and incremental innovation
  • Design and material trade-off analysis for new and legacy Airbus programmes
  • Develop solutions that meet the requirements of engineering, manufacturing, assembly, cost and time to market
  • Perform preliminary design and sizing studies using legacy methods, hand calculations and FEM
  • Validate preliminary design and suggest modifications in case of low reserve factors
  • Linear and non-linear static and fatigue stress justifications
  • Collaborate with other groups within Airframe such as materials, system installation for end to end solutions

Required Domain Expertise:

  • Strong aptitude for technology; broad technology exposure
  • Very good analytical and reasoning skills
  • Stress analysis of metallic and composite structures
  • Interpreting engineering drawings
  • Basic scripting
  • Finite element method / finite element analysis (FEM / FEA)

Tools: Patran/Nastran, Hypermesh, Abaqus, ISAMI

Qualification:

  • Graduate/Post graduate (Aerospace / Mechanical / Civil / Manufacturing)

Experience:

  • Fresher

Soft skills:

  • Ability to work independently & in a transnational team.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Flexibility & adaptability
