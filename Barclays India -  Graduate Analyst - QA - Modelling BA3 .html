About Quantitative Analytics

is led by Marco Naldi, who is a member of Risk Exco.

QA is responsible for developing, testing, implementing and supporting quantitative models for valuation and risk
management of traded assets, regulatory and economic capital, impairments, fraud detection, asset-liability
management, operational risk, net revenue and balance sheet forecasting, and stress testing across Barclays.
