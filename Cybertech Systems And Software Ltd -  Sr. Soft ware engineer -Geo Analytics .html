Job Designation

Sr. Soft ware engineer -Geo Analytics

Location

Thane

Job Description & Skills Required

Role & Responsibilities

  • Participates in design, development, enhancement, unit testing, documentation, and maintenance of Geo Centric or Geo Enabled custom business application software systems in a high performance team environment
  • Provides technical expertise in latest technologies for building, modifying and maintaining software systems in order to meet internal and external user requirements
  • Review completed tasks to ascertain compliance with standards
  • Guides or oversee the work of other technical staff and/or junior team members
  • Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies
  • Carries out analysis of problems; applies established procedures to bring resolution
  • May work on more complex projects under the direction of higher level staff or supervisor

Technical Skills

  • GIS programmer with deep analytical skills
  • Hands-on exposure to open source and/or proprietary GIS toolkits
  • Has been awarded PhD or submitted PhD thesis with research area that focuses on GIS and large spatial databases
  • Strong skills in one or more modern programming languages like C#, Java, Objective-C, JavaScript, Python and R etc. and has used it to analyze/automate/process GIS data.
  • Deep understanding of one or more of Geospatial data visualization and analytics Spatial-temporal analysis
  • Network analysis
  • Geo coding & geo referencing Data manipulation techniques
  • Ability to use machine learning techniques for spatial and non-spatial analysis using R, Python Demonstrates the ability to create, manage and maintain large databases
  • Deep understanding of the concepts behind data management in relational databases is must and knowledge of NoSQL databases is desirable
  • Contributions to any popular open source project on Github or online communities is advantageous

Required Soft skills

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Strong interpersonal skills, credibility and ability to maintain discretion
  • Effective written and verbal communication
