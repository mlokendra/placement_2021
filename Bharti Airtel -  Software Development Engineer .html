ABOUT THE ENGINEERING FUNCTION AT AIRTEL

The Engineering function at Airtel consists of people with expansive technical skills and far- reaching creativity to confront the technological challenges of the dynamic and increasingly digital market. They form the backbone of all business achievement of a common goal at the unit level and the same time they are at the forefront of the cutting edge innovation around digitization, big data, analytics and development. As a member of the Engineering team, you will strategize the complete digitization for the organization. You would get to work in the space of product engineering on technologies around big data and analytics.  This function is integral to sustain and lead in a technology driven ecosystem like ours.

ROLE: Software developer working on Product Engineering and Apps

Airtel has the largest customer base & offers scale which opens up a world of opportunities. This is every developers dream as it allows him exposure to cutting edge technology, & a chance to eventually become full stack developers with exposure on both Web & App.

Individuals will also have a chance to work on both, Airtel’s internal suite of products & on external customer facing products like My Airtel, Airtel Money, ECAF, DT etc. We foster the spirit of entrepreneurship & empowerment which gives a developer the chance to be a part of the entire product life cycle starting from identifying the problem statement to developing their own unique technical solutions.

Key work done by Engineering team in Airtel around Digital Channels and Product Engineering

  • Track user behaviors & taking real-time actions using distributed systems and data streaming stack consisting of Hadoop, Spark, Cassandra, Storm etc.
  • Personalized help & support using Elastic search and NLP based solutions.
  • We heavily use variety of NoSQL solutions to cater our variety of data persistence use cases. E.g. MongoDB, Aerospike, Cassandra
  • We follow industry best software development methodologies like Modular Development Approach, Microservices etc.
  • We practice tools like Git, Github, Bitbucket for code management and industry standard tools for continuous integration & deployment, Configurations management and infra management e.g. chef, Jenkins etc.
  • We process GBs of log data per day using Lambda architecture to understand our user better.
  • Deployed multiple industry best monitoring systems like ELK to track any failures in our production environment.
  • Teams having solid & rich experience working in both Web & App domain serving million users per day
  • We use range of tools/languages/frameworks/libraries for our software development like Spring Projects, Caching Frameworks(Redis/Aerospike),
  • Web Technologies(JS/Angular), NIO framework (Netty, Node JS), NoSQL Databases (Cassandra, MongoDB), Relational Databases(Oracle), Search Server (Solr, Elasticsearch)
  • We are agile. Our process is collaborative, transparent, use-centric and iterative.
